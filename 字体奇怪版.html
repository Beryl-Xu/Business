Tool Overview

This organizational change evaluation tool helps Volkswagen China Business Units assess the necessity and feasibility of organizational structure adjustments, staffing changes, and synergy initiatives. It provides a systematic framework to evaluate both headcount increases and centralization efforts (e.g., "One HR/Finance/IT") while ensuring strategic alignment and operational efficiency.

Key Objectives:

  • Assess organizational change necessity for business transformation
  • Evaluate both headcount addition and centralization initiatives
  • Ensure strategic alignment with regional synergy goals
  • Provide data-driven insights for HR COE prioritization
  • Facilitate self-diagnosis of organizational effectiveness
